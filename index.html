About Us

FoodShare is a platform dedicated to reducing food waste and addressing food insecurity in our communities. We connect restaurants and food businesses with surplus food to non-profit organizations and individuals in need.

Our platform makes it easy for restaurants to donate food that would otherwise go to waste, while ensuring that those donations reach people who need them most.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no food goes to waste while people go hungry. Our mission is to create a seamless connection between food surplus and food need, ensuring that good food feeds people, not landfills.

By 2030, we aim to reduce food waste by 50% in the communities we serve, while providing nutritious meals to thousands of individuals facing food insecurity.
